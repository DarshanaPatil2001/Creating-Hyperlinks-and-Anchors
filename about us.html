KRISHNA-THE SUPREME PERSONALITY OF GODHEAD

Krishna has his own identity, as does each of us. Person is not a limited concept when applied to God and absolute truth. The Vedas (ancient Indian scriptures) define Krishna as the supreme conscious being among all other conscious beings. He is infinite and we are finite. He is ultimately responsible for all creation, maintenance and destruction of the material world.
