SPIRITUALITY:

Spirituality is a broad concept of belief in something beyond the self. It seeks to answer questions about the meaning of life, how people are connected, truths about the universe, and other mysteries of human existence.Spirituality connects you with God through your own personal experience. It is primarily about discovering, experiencing and embodying your true spiritual nature.Who is considered more perfect, always properly engaged in devotional service, or worshiping the impersonal Brahman, the unmanifested?

KRISHNA-THE SUPREME PERSONALITY OF GODHEAD

Krishna has his own identity, as does each of us. Person is not a limited concept when applied to God and absolute truth. The Vedas (ancient Indian scriptures) define Krishna as the supreme conscious being among all other conscious beings. He is infinite and we are finite. He is ultimately responsible for all creation, maintenance and destruction of the material world.

The Hare Krishna mantra, also referred to reverentially as the Maha Mantra “Great Mantra”. This Mantra is composed of three Sanskrit names of the Supreme Being; “Hare,” “Krishna,” and “Rama.The three words Hare, Krishna and Rama are seed sounds of the Maha Mantra. Hare refers to this her one infinite source energy of all of us. energy around us.Rama and Krishna refer to the incarnations of Lord Vishnu.We seek this universal energy when we chant this mantra. This mantra has the power to clear our bad habits and negative emotions. By chanting or listening to mantras, we are freed from all fears and inner fears.
